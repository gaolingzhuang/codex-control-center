## [0.4.15] - 2026-07-18

### Added

- Native macOS menu bar quota display with system menu behavior
- Live Codex quota retrieval through the local app-server
- Small, medium, and large WidgetKit desktop widgets
- Today, current quota-cycle, and all-time task usage views
- Period-relative task share bars and expandable low-usage task grouping
- Pace-aware quota health labels, reset time, and reset-credit display
- Configurable automatic refresh and low-quota notifications
- Local quota history and widget snapshots
- Provider protocol for future Claude, Cursor, Gemini, and other integrations
